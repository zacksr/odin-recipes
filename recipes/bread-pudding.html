Blueberry Bread pudding

This delicius bread pudding with blueberrys is incredebly moist and easy to make.

  • 1 loaf of dense white bread, cubed (about 4-5 cups)
  • 2 cups milk (I used 1%)
  • 2 cups cream
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 pint blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon raw (turbinado) sugar*
  • 1 tablespoon butter, to coat the dish
